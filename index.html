The former president of the Galaxy: Zaphod Beeblebrox

The ex-president mixing two Pan Galactic Gargle Blasters - one for each head.

Zaphod Bebblebrox is a self-claimed adventurer, ex-hippy, and a good timer. He has been described as the "Best Bang
since the Big One" by Eccentrica Gallumbits and he was voted 'Worst dressed Sentient Being in the Known Universe'
seven consecutive times.

Further Accomplishments:
  • Owner of a highly profitable second-hand ballpoint pen business
  • Captain of the Heart of Gold
  • Creator of the Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster
